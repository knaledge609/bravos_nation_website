About

  • The Bravos were Born

    Welcome to everything you need to hear. Escape from your Reality now enter ours, The Bravos Nation.
    The Bravos formed in 2010 when two talented Producers, Oliver Grey (Mikhail Johnson) & Grammy nominated Tune Da Rula (Akintunde Akinwande), connected through colleagues, friends and a mutually shared passion to make music. Building The Bravos foundation on a combination of hard work, versatility, and creativity with their own personal touch, the group landed placements on major television networks in addition to placements with renown recording artists. .

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    In 2013, The Bravos were introduced to KnaLedge (Joseph Subero) an
    A&R at MIH Entertainment, Content Editor for HotNewHipHop.com. KnaLedge recognized the groups mission and through his experience in the music industry, came on board as a manager before transitioning himself into a Producer role in early 2014. During this transition, The Bravos recruited Shndō (Luis Martinez), their final member and talented Producer/DJ out of Miami, FL. Operating with a diverse skill set, and the knowledge/experience of the music industry, the group was able to gain attention on an international level.

  • Phase One

    Transition to Full Service

    It took one amazing record to prove that sky is the limit and together they could accomplish everything beyond what they originally envisioned. The purpose behind, “The Bravos” is the eradication of “genre” and embracing music as a whole. This is truly what makes The Bravos unique. There are no limits to their music, no rules to their methods, and nothing stopping them from becoming one of the most prominent musical groups ever. They have constantly stated that their influences are, in short, everything.

  • Phase Two

    Expansion

    Not only do they consider themselves Producers/Writers/DJs of music, but more as wielders of it, maestros of an orchestra and leaders of a movement. Vision, purpose, passion, and drive; are the factors that keep this group together. The Bravos are relentlessly working with artists of all genres and letting their music touch the souls of the people who hear it.
